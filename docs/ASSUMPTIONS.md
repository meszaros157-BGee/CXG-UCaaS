# Assumptions

## Data Access
- This site does not connect to any internal databases or APIs
- All content is statically defined within the project
- No authentication or user-specific data is required

## Strategy
- All recommendations are directional and intended to frame a conversation
- Figures, projections, and timelines are illustrative, not binding
- Content should be treated as a living document subject to refinement

## Post-Approval
- Requires validation and sign-off from relevant stakeholders before any execution
- Technical feasibility assessments should follow approval
- Budget and resourcing are outside the scope of this proposal
